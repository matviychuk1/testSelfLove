title about makeup

Natural makeup / Red carpet makeup / Evening makeup / Creative makeup / Self makeup course / Fashion makeup course

Natural makeup

This can sometimes be described as “Second Skin.” This look defines your features without major changes, sculpting, or colors.

Red carpet makeup

When it comes to red carpet makeup, there are just two criteria: first, your glow-up has to combine classic Hollywood glamor with a modern edge. Second, it has to go the distance by being smudge-proof and heat-proof, while leaving no margin for flashback when walking through crowds of photographers.

Evening makeup

Evening makeup looks call for high drama: a bit of shimmer, bright eyes that pop, and bold lips! You may want to wear sparkling or glittery eye shadow at night because it will create a nice look when the lights dance off it.

Creative makeup

Most creative makeup looks are all about color. Here is where personal preference comes into play. You can use eyeshadow palettes, loose pigments, cream shadows, glitters, and so much more to incorporate color into your look.

Self makeup course

Each makeup lesson is a 1-1 step by step teaching method with your personal Professional Makeup Artist.

Fashion makeup course

In this fast paced, competitive industry a make-up artist must be aware of current trends and new techniques. The course covers essential and classic make-up skills, fashion and photographic techniques.
