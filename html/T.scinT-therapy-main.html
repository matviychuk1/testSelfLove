A RANGE OF PROFESSIONAL SKIN THERAPIES TO REVITALISE & ENHANCE YOUR SKIN, PROVIDING OUTSTANDING RESULTS

Skin peels / Meso cocktail injections / Lipolytics / Profhilo / Facials / Microneedling / Face massage / Personalised skin care routine

Skin peels

Peels are designed to exfoliate your skin and encourage it to renew itself by creating fresh new skin cells. The powerful blend of ingredients in skin peels work to reduce the appearance of open pores, remove dead skin cells, target blemishes, acne and pigmentation, and visibly smooth fine lines and wrinkles.

Meso cocktail injections

Mesotherapy is an incredibly powerful treatment that involves tiny injections of a potent skin rejuvenating cocktail of Hyaluronic Acid, vitamins, minerals, antioxidants and amino acids. No anaesthetic required, no downtime, just a mega-watt glow in 48hours that will last for 6 weeks.

Lipolytic injections

Fat-dissolving injections (injection lipolysis) can effectively eliminate the fat from stubborn areas of the face, such as the chin for example. The effect is visible within four-six weeks of the first session and usually lasts for a long time if proper measures are taken.

Personalised skin care routine

Beautiful Skin Doesn’t Come From Guess Work. Take control of your skin with products you can't get over-the-counter.

Facials

Facial is a beautifying skin treatment that cleanses pores, exfoliates dead skin cells, hydrates and moisturises the skin, and treats common skin concerns with a customised regime. Facials are designed to boost the immediate appearance and overall health and longevity of the skin. 

Microneedling

Microneedling is a cosmetic procedure. It involves pricking the skin with tiny sterilized needles. The small wounds cause your body to make more collagen and elastin, which heal your skin and help you look younger. You might also hear it called collagen induction therapy.

Face massage

Facial massage is a cosmetic treatment that includes using hands or a tool to massage the face and neck to stimulate blood circulation, promote relaxation, and improve the appearance of the skin. Facial massage represents rejuvenating treatment that can help you achieve a more youthful, glowing complexion.
